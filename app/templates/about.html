About Shannon Glamping

Nestled on the serene banks of the Shannon Estuary, Shannon Glamping offers a unique blend of comfort and nature.

We believe in creating unforgettable outdoor experiences while ensuring every guest enjoys the luxury of a cozy, eco-friendly stay. Whether you're a couple seeking a romantic getaway or a family yearning for a nature-filled adventure, we have the perfect retreat for you.

Our accommodations are designed with sustainability in mind, using local materials and offering stunning views of the surrounding landscape.
