About Our Glamping Haven

Welcome to your escape — a luxury glamping experience nestled in the heart of West Limerick. Whether you're seeking adventure, rest, or reconnection, our site offers a one-of-a-kind stay surrounded by nature, history, and comfort.

Our Story

Born from a love of the wild outdoors and the comfort of cozy living, our glampsite was created as a haven for those looking to unwind, recharge, and immerse themselves in natural beauty. With stunning views of the historic Shanid Castle and access to the scenic Limerick Greenway, our location is perfect for both relaxation and exploration.

What We Offer

  • Luxury Yurts, Eco Cabins, and Treehouses
  • Private fire pits, outdoor dining, and starry skies
  • Shower and bathroom facilities
  • On-site parking and complimentary local guides
  • Walking and cycling access to the Limerick Greenway

Sustainability Commitment

We’re proud to be eco-conscious. Our accommodations are built with sustainability in mind, using renewable energy sources, natural materials, and thoughtful design to minimize our impact and celebrate the land we live on.

Meet Your Hosts

We're a family run business who love our home and want to share its magic with others. From the welcome basket to local tips, we’re here to make your stay unforgettable.
